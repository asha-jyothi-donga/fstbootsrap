Beaut was conceived, in 2010 by blahblah, as a melting pot of passions, personalities and different ways of design thinking. We are an agile practice of architects and designers that hail from all over the globe.



We create immersive, multi-sensory and seductive environments for our clients and have created more than 1,500 projects for global brands in the worlds of retail, lifestyle and hospitality.



The Beaut team is united by an appreciation for the finer things in life, each member adding their own flavour as to how Beaut thinks, acts and works but all sharing the desire to create spaces that are tactile, intimate and encourage interaction.
